# urticare
Built with a gentle, non-clinical approach, UrtiCare combines emotional support, smart tracking, and pattern awareness into one clean experience that helps people living with chronic urticaria feel more in control of their body and mind.
The app allows users to:

Track consumables, medications, drinks, smoke exposure, and daily habits
Log symptoms and flare intensity
Detect recurring triggers and behavioral patterns through the Pattern Hub
Build personalized quick selections for faster daily logging
Navigate stress through guided breathing and emotional support tools
Interact with Urti, a calm AI well-being guardian designed to provide grounding, reassurance, and emotional balance during stressful moments

Rather than overwhelming users with medical complexity, UrtiCare focuses on clarity, emotional comfort, and actionable self-awareness. The experience is intentionally soft, minimal, and easy on the mind — helping users recognize what affects them physically and emotionally over time.

UrtiCare is not just a tracker.
It is a personal well-being space for people trying to reconnect the dots between stress, habits, and their body’s reactions.
